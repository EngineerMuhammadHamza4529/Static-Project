About Navaratri

Navaratri is a vibrant Hindu festival celebrated for nine nights, dedicated to the worship of the goddess Durga. It marks the triumph of good over evil and symbolizes the power of the divine feminine. During Navaratri, people engage in traditional dances like Garba and Dandiya, worship the goddess, and participate in festive processions. It is a time of joy, devotion, and cultural celebrations that bring communities together.
